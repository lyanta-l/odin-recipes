Scrambled eggs with tomatoes

Description

Scrambled Eggs with Tomatoes is a beloved Chinese home-style dish, cherished for its vibrant color, refreshing taste, and simplicity. It's a quick and easy stir-fry that balances the savory richness of eggs with the slight tanginess and sweetness of ripe tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Large Eggs
  • 2 Medium Ripe Tomatoes, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil (or more, as needed)
  • 1 tsp Salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar (optional, to enhance tomato sweetness)
  • 1 tbsp Water or Shaoxing Wine (optional, for fluffier eggs)
  • Chopped Green Onions or Cilantro for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add salt and water/Shaoxing wine (if using). Beat well with chopsticks or a whisk until the yolks and whites are fully combined and slightly frothy.
  2. Prepare the tomatoes: Wash and chop the tomatoes into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Scramble the eggs: Heat 1 tbsp of cooking oil in a wok or non-stick pan over medium-high heat. Pour in the beaten eggs. Let them set for a few seconds, then gently stir and scramble until they are just cooked but still slightly moist. Remove the scrambled eggs from the wok and set aside.
  4. Stir-fry tomatoes: Add the remaining 1 tbsp of cooking oil to the wok (add more if needed). Add the chopped tomatoes and stir-fry over medium-high heat for about 2-3 minutes, until they start to soften and release their juices.
  5. Season the tomatoes: Add salt and sugar (if using) to the tomatoes. Stir well.
  6. Combine: Return the scrambled eggs to the wok with the tomatoes. Gently stir and mix everything together for about 30 seconds to 1 minute, until the tomatoes are coated in egg and the flavors have melded.
  7. Serve: Transfer the dish to a serving plate. Garnish with chopped green onions or cilantro if desired. Serve hot with steamed rice.
